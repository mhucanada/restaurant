About Us

Food made for you
We are an online food service focused on bringing our community fast, delicious food right to your doorstep.
We use only the highest quality ingredients, from our local farms.
Throughout the years, we have seen a grwoth in the number of online food services, but none of them were as fresh as we would have desired.
Our founder, Halldór Viktor Ármannsson saw this, and in 1666, created this service in hopes to improve the dining experience.
